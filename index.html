About MoonChen

We deal in very important inputs of modern agricultural technology aimed at doubling the productivity of cereals, pulses, cotton, horticultural and vegetable crops. Most of its products are of organic origin. This include pesticides, plant growth promotors and stimulators, growth hormones, root system developers and soil amendments.

Company has in its ambit, pesticides that are contact, stomach and systemic ones, killing, chewing as well as sucking insects namely caterpillars, aphids, jassids, mealy bugs, thrips etc. These can be used as foliar spray or mixed with fertiliser for soil application.
